## Source Video
- Title: How to Send 10,000 Cold Emails a Day (Easy Tutorial)
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=trkZrJ83jEc

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Scale cold email only after setting up proper sending infrastructure (domains, inboxes, warm-up, deliverability).
2. Separate infrastructure quality from copy quality so optimization is data-driven.
3. Use strong lead targeting and verification to protect deliverability and improve reply quality.
4. Monitor campaign health continuously (bounce rate, inbox placement, reply quality).
5. Treat scale as an operations problem: systems, QA checks, and ongoing iteration.

## B2B SaaS Application
- Start with low-volume pilot campaigns before scaling.
- Use domain rotation and warmed inboxes for safer growth.
- Pair volume with tight segmentation to avoid low-intent responses.
