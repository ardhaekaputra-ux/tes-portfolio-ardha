# Anthony Iannarino - Strategy Notes

## Source Video
- Title: The 17 minute Cold Call Course for B2B Sales
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=17SF_CBE2Pg

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Use outreach to create value in the first interaction, not to push for immediate product demos.
2. Open conversations with insight and business relevance instead of generic introductions.
3. Ask for specific commitments and define clear next steps to move opportunities forward.
4. Replace scripts with structured talk tracks that keep conversations natural and buyer-focused.
5. Use multi-touch outreach (email + phone) with consistent messaging and clear outcomes.

## B2B SaaS Application
- Equip SDRs with insight-led opening statements by vertical.
- Standardize CTA language for discovery and executive briefings.
- Use call outcomes to improve future email personalization.
