# Charlie Morgan Business - Strategy Notes

## Source Video
- Title: BEST SMMA Cold Email Sequence To Get Clients 2025
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kp1i10ucVRc

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Build sequences around one clear market problem and one transformation promise.
2. Use short, direct cold emails with clear language and no unnecessary formatting.
3. Follow up with angle variation: pain reminder, case-study proof, and opportunity-cost framing.
4. Prioritize message-market fit over copy tricks; weak offers cannot be fixed by better phrasing.
5. Use sequence consistency so response data can identify winning angles quickly.

## B2B SaaS Application
- Convert "agency" framing into SaaS-specific value propositions by ICP.
- Test one offer per sequence for cleaner performance insights.
- Use replies to refine objection handling and next campaign iterations.
