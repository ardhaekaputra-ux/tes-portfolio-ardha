# Connor Murray - Strategy Notes

## Source Video
- Title: The Best B2B Sales Prospecting Techniques For SDR/BDRs in 2026
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yme7MspfkRg

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Prospecting performance depends on role clarity, daily structure, and activity quality.
2. Build account and contact lists with strict ICP filters before launching outreach.
3. Use value statements tied to common buyer outcomes, then tailor by title and pain point.
4. Run multi-channel cadences and track what creates conversations, not just activity volume.
5. Improve through regular call/email review loops and objection pattern analysis.

## B2B SaaS Application
- Define outbound playbooks for SDR coverage by segment.
- Standardize first-touch messaging around business outcomes.
- Use sequence analytics to refine persona-specific messaging.
