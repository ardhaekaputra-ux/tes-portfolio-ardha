# Lead Gen Jay - Strategy Notes

## Source Video
- Title: How to Build a Lead Gen MACHINE With Cold Email
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PwbuufgVwn8

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Treat cold email as a system: list building, infrastructure, copy, sending logic, and reply handling.
2. Build clean sending infrastructure (domains, inboxes, deliverability setup) before scaling volume.
3. Segment lead lists tightly so each campaign speaks to one pain, one persona, and one offer.
4. Keep copy concise and outcome-driven: short hook, problem context, proof, and simple CTA.
5. Iterate weekly based on reply quality, positive response rate, and booked meetings.

## B2B SaaS Application
- Create campaign pods by vertical and buyer role.
- Standardize follow-up logic for non-responders.
- Track quality metrics, not just send volume.
