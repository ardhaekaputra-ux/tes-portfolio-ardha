# Michael Humblet - Strategy Notes

## Source Video
- Title: Mastering The Ultimate Cold Outreach Sales Strategy
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2d7NNAAZOpI

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Start outreach with relevance, not a pitch. Use referral context or a credible trigger to earn attention.
2. Lead with the prospect's business problem and create "constructive tension" around current risk/cost.
3. Prove outcomes with concrete examples (case studies, quantified impact, specific customer scenarios).
4. Ask for a low-friction next step (short call, quick audit, short Loom), not a large commitment.
5. Run outreach as a sequence across email and LinkedIn touchpoints so messages compound over time.

## B2B SaaS Application
- Build outreach by ICP and pain category.
- Personalize first line with context, then move quickly to a value hypothesis.
- Use short sequences that rotate proof points and keep CTA lightweight.
