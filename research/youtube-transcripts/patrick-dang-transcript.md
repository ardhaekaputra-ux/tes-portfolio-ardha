# Patrick Dang - Strategy Notes

## Source Video
- Title: TOP 5 Cold Email Tips to DOMINATE B2B Sales
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W-znS2Tkl8o

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Validate the offer first because low response often signals weak positioning, not only poor copy.
2. Write subject lines and opening lines that reflect the buyer's world, not generic sales language.
3. Keep cold emails brief and easy to answer with one clear next action.
4. Use follow-ups as value reminders and timing checks, not repetitive "just checking in" messages.
5. Pair outreach execution with measurable process discipline (cadence, testing, and iteration).

## B2B SaaS Application
- Audit product-market messaging before scaling sends.
- Use role-specific language for founders, sales leaders, and ops leaders.
- Standardize follow-up windows by deal complexity.
