# Rob Walling - Strategy Notes

## Source Video
- Title: If I Started SaaS in 2026, Here's My B2B Content Strategy for $1M ARR
- URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Sxn1ji7vYzU

## Core Strategy Summary
1. Build acquisition around customer awareness stages instead of publishing random content.
2. Align messaging with where buyers are: problem-aware, solution-aware, or product-aware.
3. Use educational content to warm demand so outbound outreach converts at higher rates.
4. Focus on compounding channels (content + email capture + nurturing) rather than one-off tactics.
5. Treat inbound and outbound as a single pipeline system with shared ICP and positioning.

## B2B SaaS Application
- Map content to the same ICP segments used in cold outreach.
- Feed outbound campaigns with insights from top-performing inbound topics.
- Use lead nurture to reduce friction before sales conversations.
